About this simulation

This simulator uses physics-based models to approximate the behavior of a ZF beamforming system under varying channel conditions. When channel instability (measured by CCI) exceeds the switching threshold, WaveLynk proactively transitions to MRT beamforming to prevent link collapse.

What it demonstrates: predictive switching detects instability before the link degrades, maintaining throughput and latency even as conditions worsen.

What it is not: a full PHY-layer link simulator. The mappings from CCI to SNR/throughput are physics-inspired approximations, not bit-accurate simulations.
